## Framework: Expansion of Awareness Practice

### Principle
Worth feeds Partnership feeds Vision feeds Worth. The loop doesn't end — it circulates. Each layer strengthens the next.

### Daily Practice — Moon/Foundations (5 min)

**"What do I have? What do I know?"**

Morning inventory. Not gratitude journaling — honest assessment:
- Resources: time, money, energy, skills. Where do they stand?
- Worth: what am I bringing to the table today that has real value?
- Awareness check: what did I learn yesterday that I didn't know before?

Track in daily note or Sunsama morning ritual.

*Ki connection: Kūn (Earth/Receptivity) → the ground you stand on before you move.*

### Weekly Practice — Venus/Encounter (30 min, Fridays)

**"Who did I meet? What did we build?"**

One deliberate act of collaboration or connection per week:
- Client call, creative conversation, reaching out to someone you admire
- Review: did this week's partnerships reflect my actual values?
- Track in **Links** sprint channel

Quality questions:
- Did I show up authentically in my collaborations this week?
- Where did I compromise my worth for convenience?
- What partnership moved my vision forward?

*Ki connection: Zhèn (Thunder/Movement) → the initiative that breaks through inertia.*

### Monthly Review — Jupiter/Vision (1 hour, first weekend)

**"Is my network growing toward the vision, or just growing?"**

Strategic expansion check:
- One move toward the bigger picture (passive income, astrology tools, community)
- Review: what compounded this month? What stalled?
- Prune: what partnerships or projects drained without returning?

Profection year reminder: Jupiter as Lord of Year = expansion through trust, not effort. Don't force the network. Let quality compound.

*Ki connection: Hexagram 55 (Expansion of Awareness) → clarity + movement. Act from understanding, not anxiety.*

### Quarterly Recalibration

**"What illusion did I lose this quarter?"**

Hex 55's core teaching: a loss of illusion IS a gain in awareness.
- What did I believe 3 months ago that I now know was wrong?
- How has that changed what I'm building?
- Update the framework based on what you've actually learned.
